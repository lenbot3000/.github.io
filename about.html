Claim Access to your Artist Profiles

Edit your Spotify / Apple profiles

      If you're making a new artist profile for therestisnoise, you will need to claim
      your artist profiles in order to customise them on streaming services.

      Here's how to claim your Spotify profile:
      1. artists.spotify.com
      2. Click “Get Access”. We can provide your Spotify URI/URL if you are a new artist and your
      profile isn't live yet. Just ask us.
      3. Log on using your own personal Spotify user account
      4. Click 'artist/manager', or 'label' if you are a label

      Key Info; You will need the UPC, social media link and profile URI/URI.
      which we can provide - just send mia@gyrostream.com or alex@gyrostream.com
      an email asking for this and I'll send it your way.

      If you are claiming a second artist in your Spotify For Artists dashboard, you need to:

      1. Click on the three dots in the top left
      2. Click your email address in the bottom left
      3. From there you then click 'Add Artist'
      4. Fill out the application as per normal.

      GYROstream also has a handy a blog post about claiming more artist pages
      (including Apple Music), which is available here.
